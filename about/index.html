✦ once upon a time ✦

The Whimsiverse

Deep within the space between imagination and dreams lives a world called the Whimsiverse — a magical realm where Imaginary Friends come to life, go on adventures, and remind us of the things that matter most.

The Whimsiverse is home to creatures of all kinds: brave adventurers, gentle souls, creative dreamers, and curious wonderers. Each one carries a piece of the magic that lives in every child's heart — and the heart of every adult who remembers.

Their stories are for every heart that ever forgot how to dream.

✦ behind the magic ✦

Meet Sachi

🎨

Sachi

Illustrator · Whimsiverse Creator · Dream Builder

Hi, I'm Sachi — a digital illustrator from Sri Lanka and the creator of the Whimsiverse. ✦

I made Woobly & Friends because I believe the world needs more magic. More colour. More of that feeling you get when you're a child and anything is possible.

My art is a reminder that your inner child is still alive — it's just waiting for a reason to come out and play. Every character I draw is a little piece of that reminder.

I draw everything in Procreate on my iPad. Every Whimsiverse piece starts as a dream and ends as something you can hold in your hands. 🌸

Gregy

Curious, a little sleepy, and finds wonder in the smallest things. Gregy's favourite place is a lily pad — but he'll follow his friends anywhere.
