✦ the origin ✦

The Whimsiverse

Okay, imagine this…

Remember your imaginary friend from when you were little?
Yeah… they didn't disappear. They just moved.

Welcome to Whimsiverse — a magical reality built entirely from imagination.

Every imaginary friend ever created lives here now — Wooblies, sleepy froggys, teddy bears, unicorns… all of them.

It's like the hidden opposite of our world.
While we deal with life… Whimsiverse stays magical.

And the first to ever call it home?

The Wooblies. ✨

Okay, this story starts with Fluffy the Woobly.

Fluffy is the most playful, energetic Woobly ever — he just can't stay in one place. So one day, he decides… why not explore the whole Whimsiverse?

He hops onto his spray-paint rocket (yeah, it's as cool as it sounds) and zooms across magical worlds.

Along the way, he meets new friends — Mimi, Bella and Benny, Greggy, and so many more.

And honestly? That's what this is all about.

So come join Fluffy and his friends…
make new friends, collect their stories, and feel the magic. ✨

✦ behind the magic ✦

Meet Sachi

🎨

Sachindu

Guardian of Whimsiverse · Digital Illustrator · Dream Builder

Hi… I'm Sachindu.

Not just an artist.
Not just a creator.

I'm the Guardian of Whimsiverse… the one who writes it into existence.

Every Woobly, every sleepy froggy, every soft little story you see — they all begin as a tiny spark in my imagination… and then I bring them to life.

In your world, I'm a digital illustrator. A student who loves creating children's book art and soft, happy things.

But beyond this reality? I shape a universe.

I created Woobly & Friends because I felt something was missing… More magic. More colour. More of that feeling you had as a child — when anything felt possible.

Whimsiverse is that feeling… given a place to live.

It's where your inner child never disappeared — just waiting… quietly… for a reason to come out and play.

So if this world ever feels too heavy, step inside mine.
Be yourself. Rest a little. Enjoy the whimsy.

Everything here begins as a dream…
and somehow… ends up in your hands. 🌸

Woobly & Friends

Fluffy the Woobly

So first… you already know Fluffy. The most playful Woobly out there — the one who just couldn't sit still.

Bella & Benny

✦ Greenwillow Isle

In a soft corner of the Whimsiverse called Greenwillow Isle, you'll find Bella and Benny — a gentle teddy bear couple who believe one simple thing: kindness can change everything.

So they travel, help, and quietly make the world a little warmer.

Mimi the Unicorn

✦ Luminella Isle

If you ever reach the glowing lands of Luminella Isle, you might meet Mimi — a unicorn princess with a magical heart companion.

Together, they don't just spread love… they remind others what it feels like.

Greggy the Frog

From Puddlehop Isle, where things are always a little splashy and unpredictable. He's clumsy, a little chaotic… but honestly? The group wouldn't be the same without him.

Because wherever Greggy goes — laughs follow.
