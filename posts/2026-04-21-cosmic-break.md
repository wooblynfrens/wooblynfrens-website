**For many days, Fluffy the Woobly, Benny the Bear, and Gregy the Frog had been traveling across the Whimsiverse.
They wandered through glowing skies, met kind strangers, and found adventure in every corner they visited.
Wherever they went, they left a little happiness behind.**

**But even the most joyful journeys can be tiring.**

**One quiet evening, they came upon a gentle cosmic river, where tiny stars floated like lights upon the water.
The three friends drifted along without a word.
Gregy soon fell asleep, carried by the calm.
Benny closed his eyes and said he was only resting.
Fluffy simply watched the stars, smiling softly.**

**There were no plans to follow and no places to reach.
Only a peaceful moment, shared together.**

**And in that quiet stillness, they remembered that rest is not the end of an adventure,
but a gentle part of it.**
