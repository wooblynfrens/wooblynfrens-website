Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Welcome to Woobly & Friends (wooblynfrens.com). Your privacy matters to us — and honestly, we keep things pretty simple here. This page explains what we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Who We Are

Woobly & Friends is an independent art brand created by Sachindu ("Sachi"), based in Sri Lanka. We sell digital and physical art products through our shop (Payhip) and run a monthly mail club through Patreon.

Contact: hello@wooblynfrens.com

2. What Information We Collect

When you visit the website

We don't directly collect personal information from visitors just browsing the site. However, standard web hosting services (Netlify) may log basic technical data like IP addresses and page visit timestamps for security and performance purposes.

When you subscribe to the newsletter

If you sign up for our Mailerlite mailing list, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Any optional name you provide

This is used only to send you the Woobly & Friends newsletter. We never share or sell your email address. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.

When you make a purchase

Purchases are handled by Payhip. When you buy something, Payhip collects your email address, payment information, and delivery address (for physical items). We only receive your email and shipping details to fulfill physical orders. See Payhip's Privacy Policy for full details.

When you support us on Patreon

Patreon handles all billing and subscription management. We receive your shipping address for physical tier rewards. See Patreon's Privacy Policy for full details.

3. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Some third-party embeds (Mailerlite, Payhip) may set their own cookies when you interact with them. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To send newsletter emails you opted into
  • To fulfill physical orders (shipping address)
  • To communicate about your orders or Patreon membership

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

Email lists are stored securely with Mailerlite. Shipping addresses provided for physical orders are kept only as long as needed to fulfill your order. We take reasonable precautions but cannot guarantee absolute security of any internet transmission.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@wooblynfrens.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out:
